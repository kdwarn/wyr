Welcome!

I built What You've Read to help me you keep track of the books, articles, blog posts, and other things Iyou've read and saved in various programs and places across the internet, such as:
  • Mendeley
  • Goodreads
  • Social bookmarking sites
  • Browser bookmarks
What You've Read will connect to the places you authorize, get the things it finds there, and combine them into one simplified collection, providing a scrolling list of all the things you've read - a reading journal. Browse it all, search, or sort by tags and authors. Revisit the item by clicking on a link or opening at the service you originally saved it in. You can also add things to What You've Read natively, by submitting a short form (accessible via bookmarklet or directly through the site), to catch those blog posts or podcasts you aren't currently saving elsewhere.

Sign up and try it out. It's very much in beta, and thus far only supports Mendeley, Goodreads, and the native adding, but I'm working to expand it. You can contact me here.
