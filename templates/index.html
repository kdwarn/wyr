Welcome!

I built What You've Read to help me you keep track of the books, articles, blog posts, and other things I you've read and saved in various places across the internet.

In addition to gathering scholarly articles from Mendeley and books from Goodreads, it can also import your browser bookmarks and be used as a reference manager/bookmarking site itself. It will display all of the things you've read from the places you choose in one simplified, chronological collection - a reading journal - which you can search or sort by tags and authors.

It's free and there are no ads. See screenshots here. Contact me here. See Github here.
