Bookmarklet

If you want to be able to quickly add things (individually) to What You've Read, drag this link (wyr) to your bookmarks bar and click it anytime you are on a page you want to save. It will open the form (with title and URL pre-populated) to add a new item. Then, just fill in any additional information (like author, year, and tags) and submit it.

Also note: What You've Read will only check for updates you make every three hours. So if you loaded the site, made a change on one of these services, and then immediately re-load to see the change, it won't be there for three hours. This is because it takes a lot of time to check for changes. A force-refresh is in the works.
